What We Do

Here at Pensacola High School, we thrive to create an easy-to-navigate, minimal website for all students and parents to access when an item has gone missing.

Universal System

No more inquires about who or where to report loss items or recover a found item. At our website, we provide a platform for everybody to access while also prioritizing a better customer service experience.

Response Time

This service provides a real time email alert upon reporting or recovering items! We allow students and teachers to report loss items all from their phone or computer. They will be required to submit a form where our supervisors can validate and respond in accordance to the item conveniently.

Verification

Supervisors returning found items to their original owners can verify their authentification through our claim process. This creates a reassuring method for an incident where an item may have wrongly been returned to the wrong owner.
